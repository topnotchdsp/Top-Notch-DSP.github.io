About Us

Diverse experience in software and electrical engineering

My professional experience includes software quality assurance, information technology installation and administration,
STEM education, industrial electrical engineering, and now CNC manufacturing.

After studying Electrical and Computer Engineering at Western Washington University, I began a journey into freelancing which eventually opened doors into motion control and audio processing.

I began learning audio programming and practical Digital Signal Processing (DSP). I started using the JUCE framework,
and sketching circuits that could run embedded code and communicate with applications. Surely there was a demand for
a skilled, agile developer who understood hardware and software development?

I am now working hard to develop my technical skills in a cluster of Digital Signal Processing fields
including software, hardware, and networking.
